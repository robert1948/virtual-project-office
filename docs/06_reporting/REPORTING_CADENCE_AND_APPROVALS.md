# Reporting Cadence and Approvals

## Purpose

Define reporting schedule, minimum content, and approval flow for weekly,
monthly, and founder review outputs.

## Cadence

- Weekly report: Every Friday by end of day
- Monthly report: Last business day of each month
- Founder review pack: First business day of each month, or ad hoc for major
  decision points

## Ownership

- Report Owner: Robert
- Reviewer: Governance owner
- Final Approver for founder review: Robert

## Minimum Reporting Controls

- Include report ID and reporting period
- Include plan and work-order references
- Include decision, risk, and change references where applicable
- Include evidence links for claimed outcomes
- Include explicit next actions with owners and due dates

## Approval Flow

1. Draft report using standard template.
2. Validate required sections and evidence links.
3. Review for accuracy and governance alignment.
4. Approve and publish in the corresponding reporting folder.

## Escalation Triggers

Escalate to founder review immediately if any condition occurs:

- High-risk item reaches score 15 or higher
- Critical change request is proposed
- Major milestone slips by more than one reporting period
- Delivery confidence drops to red for any high-priority initiative
