# Cape Project Office Charter

## Purpose

The Cape Project Office exists to provide a central control layer for
CapeControl and related ventures. Its role is to turn strategy into structured
execution, maintain governance across projects, reduce fragmentation, and build
a repeatable operating model that can scale with responsible AI assistance.

## Mission

To build a trusted, evidence-first Project Office that turns strategy into
structured execution for CapeControl and related ventures, using disciplined
governance and responsible AI assistance to create lasting business value.

## Vision

To create a founder-led operating system for business building where human
judgment and AI-assisted execution work together to plan, deliver, and scale
ventures with trust, speed, and accountability.

## Scope

The Project Office supports:

- portfolio planning
- governance and decision control
- work-order management
- documentation standards
- venture coordination
- shared operating templates
- AI workforce design and oversight
- reporting, review, and risk visibility

## Authority Chain

Robert → CapeAI → VS_Chat → Codex

## Meaning

- Robert is the final authority
- CapeAI provides planning and governance guidance
- VS_Chat acts as manager and orchestrator
- Codex acts as worker and implementer

## Operating Principles

- Evidence-first execution
- Clear scope before action
- Small, reversible work orders
- Staging before production
- No production deployment or release without Robert’s explicit approval
- Shared standards across ventures
- AI assistance must remain governed, reviewable, and accountable

## Primary Functions

### 1. Governance

Maintain standards, authority boundaries, and decision discipline.

### 2. Portfolio Control

Track ventures, priorities, risks, dependencies, and major milestones.

### 3. Delivery Management

Convert goals into work orders, acceptance criteria, and evidence-backed progress.

### 4. Shared Services

Provide templates, SOPs, review patterns, and reusable systems across ventures.

### 5. AI Workforce Enablement

Design and manage an AI-supported operating model that increases execution
capacity without losing control or trust.

## Success Measures

The Project Office is succeeding when:

- priorities are visible and current
- ventures are tracked consistently
- work is translated into structured execution
- decisions and risks are documented
- AI support increases capacity without reducing governance
- founder overload is reduced through better systems

## Initial Deliverables

- Portfolio Master Plan
- Venture Register
- Work Order Register
- Risk Register
- Governance Model
- AI Workforce Model
- Founder Review Cadence
- Shared Templates and SOPs

## Commitment

The Project Office exists to build with discipline, guide with clarity, and
scale with trust.
