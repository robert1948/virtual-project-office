# Weekly Governance Review Checklist

## SOP ID

SOP-GOV-2026-0314-001

## Purpose

Provide a repeatable weekly review flow to maintain governance compliance,
risk visibility, and evidence quality.

## Cadence

- Run once per week after weekly report publication.

## Inputs

- Latest weekly report in `docs/06_reporting/weekly/`
- Active work orders in `docs/03_work_orders/active/`
- Recent changes, decisions, and risks in `logs/`
- Latest checkpoint audit sample in `docs/04_operations/SOPs/`

## Checklist

- [ ] Weekly report published with evidence links.
- [ ] Active work-order statuses reviewed.
- [ ] New change records linked to corresponding work orders.
- [ ] New decision records include evidence and follow-up actions.
- [ ] New risk records include owner, score, and review date.
- [ ] One Major/Critical work order sampled for checkpoint compliance.
- [ ] Follow-up actions due in next 7 days are confirmed with owners.

## Output

- Review notes in meeting artifacts or weekly report.
- New remediation actions assigned with owners and due dates.
