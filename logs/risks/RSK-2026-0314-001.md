# RSK-2026-0314-001 KPI Definition Drift

- Date Identified: 2026-03-14
- Category: Delivery
- Description: KPI formulas or thresholds may diverge across reports if changes
  are made without coordinated approval.
- Trigger: Weekly or monthly reports use KPI names/values that differ from the
  approved baseline.
- Impact: 3
- Likelihood: 3
- Score: 9
- Owner: Robert
- Mitigation: Require decision-log entry and approval for any KPI formula,
  threshold, or ownership change.
- Contingency: Run reconciliation review and republish corrected KPI snapshot
  with explicit correction note.

- Status: Active
