# DEC-2026-0314-003 Approve Weekly KPI Metric Snippet Automation

- Date: 2026-03-14
- Status: Approved
- Owner: Robert
- Approver: Robert
- Area: Portfolio
- Summary: Approved a lightweight automation utility for weekly KPI metric
  snippet generation.

## Context

Weekly KPI report preparation required repeated manual metric calculations,
creating avoidable effort and potential inconsistency.

## Decision

Adopt `scripts/generate_weekly_kpi_snapshot.py` and `make kpi-weekly` as the
standard helper to generate weekly plan-level KPI metrics.

## Rationale

Improves repeatability and accelerates weekly reporting while preserving a
simple, auditable implementation.

## Impact

- Scope impact: Adds KPI helper automation command and runbook integration.
- Timeline impact: Effective immediately.
- Risk impact: Reduces manual metric entry variance.
- Cost impact: No direct financial cost.
