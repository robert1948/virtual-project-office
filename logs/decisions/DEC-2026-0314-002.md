# DEC-2026-0314-002 Approve Change-Control Review Checkpoint

- Date: 2026-03-14
- Status: Approved
- Owner: Robert
- Approver: Robert
- Area: Governance
- Summary: Approved a mandatory pre-execution checkpoint for Major/Critical
  changes.

## Context

`PLN-2026-005` required an explicit control to ensure all Major changes are
linked to approved change records before execution.

## Decision

Adopt a mandatory pre-execution checkpoint in change-control policy and work
order evidence fields, with pass/fail criteria defined in operations standards.

## Rationale

Prevents uncontrolled execution, increases traceability, and improves approval
discipline for high-impact changes.

## Impact

- Scope impact: Major/Critical changes now require checkpoint pass before work.
- Timeline impact: Effective immediately from approval date.
- Risk impact: Reduces unapproved change execution risk.
- Cost impact: No direct financial cost.
