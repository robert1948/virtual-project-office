# DEC-2026-0313-004 Approve Reporting Cadence and Template Set

- Date: 2026-03-13
- Status: Approved
- Owner: Robert
- Approver: Robert
- Area: Reporting
- Summary: Approved weekly, monthly, and founder review templates with a
  unified reporting cadence and approval flow.

## Context

Reporting folders existed but lacked a defined cadence and approved template
set.

## Decision

Adopt the new reporting cadence document and three standardized templates as
mandatory format for reporting outputs.

## Rationale

Improves comparability across periods and supports decision-ready reporting.

## Impact

- Scope impact: All reporting outputs must use standardized templates.
- Timeline impact: Effective immediately.
- Risk impact: Lower risk of incomplete founder reporting.
- Cost impact: No direct cost.
